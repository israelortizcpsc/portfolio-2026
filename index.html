Signature project

Lumen NPI

Cold-start demand intelligence for high-SKU retail

Forecasts first-90-day demand for brand-new SKUs that have zero sales history — from product attributes alone — then classifies movers, flags stock risk, explains every prediction, and answers a planner's questions in plain English.

quantile forecasting (P10/P50/P90) classification · movers clustering · archetypes anomaly · stock-risk flags SHAP · per-prediction why LLM copilot · grounded + cited Power BI–style dashboard

Why it matters: a retailer launching thousands of new SKUs a year lives or dies on forecasting demand for products with no history — buy too much and you discount it, too little and you lose the sale. Lumen NPI is a working answer to exactly that problem.
